The Bar Chart component displays salary totals on the Y-axis and departments on the X-axis. Before you add the Bar Chart component, you add a custom field to the Department business object that aggregates the total salary for the department.

Add a Field to the Department Business Object to Aggregate Total Salary

Add a totalSalary field of type Number to the Department business object to aggregate data from the Employee business objectâ€™s Salary field and set the aggregation function to Total.

 Create Bar Chart Component
  1. In the Component Palette, drag a Bar Chart component and drop it onto the Page Designer.
  2. In the Quick Start tab of the Properties pane, use the Add Data quick start to bind the chart to data as follows:
    • Select the Department business object.
    • Add totalSalary to the Y Axis.
    • Add departmentName to the X Axis and the Series fields.
    • In the Define Query page, enter the expression totalSalary > 0 for the q uriParameter so that the chart does not display entries for departments with a total salary less than or equal to 0.
  3. In the General tab of the Properties pane, select On for Stack Values.
